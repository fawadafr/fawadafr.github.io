Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how lab.fawad.ai ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information through Google Analytics 4:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, navigation patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type (desktop/mobile/tablet)
  • Referrer Information: The website or link that brought you to our site
  • Interaction Events: Clicks on email links, social media links, and external links
  • Geographic Data: Approximate location based on IP address (city/country level)

Analytics Provider

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about website usage.

Google Analytics Property ID: G-YSLJNR3DWB

How We Use This Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Understand which content resonates with our audience
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Analyze traffic patterns and visitor demographics
  • Track engagement with our content and calls-to-action
  • Make data-driven decisions about content strategy

Data Sharing

Your data is shared with Google as part of the Google Analytics service. Google processes this data according to their Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique users and track sessions. These cookies typically expire after 2 years. The main cookies used are:

  • _ga: Used to distinguish users (expires in 2 years)
  • _ga_*: Used to persist session state (expires in 2 years)

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following options regarding data collection:

  • Opt-Out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Browser Settings: Configure your browser to block or delete cookies
  • Do Not Track: Enable Do Not Track (DNT) signals in your browser

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default. After this period, aggregated data is kept for historical analysis, but individual user data is deleted.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States, where Google's servers are located.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: fawad@lab.fawad.ai
