Platform Engineering Foundations: Building Developer Platforms That Scale

Jun 15, 2024

platform-engineeringdevopsdeveloper-experienceinfrastructure

Platform engineering represents the evolution of DevOps, focusing on building self-service internal developer platforms that abstract infrastructure complexity.

Core Principles

  1. Developer self-service: Enable teams to provision infrastructure without tickets
  2. Golden paths: Provide opinionated, secure, pre-approved workflows
  3. Platform as product: Treat internal platforms with product thinking

Key Components

  • Infrastructure as Code: Terraform, Pulumi, CloudFormation
  • CI/CD pipelines: GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, CircleCI
  • Observability: OpenTelemetry, Prometheus, Grafana
  • Service catalogs: Backstage, Port, Internal developer portals

Platform engineering reduces time-to-production while maintaining governance and security.
